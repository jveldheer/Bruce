NUTRITION STRATEGY

Daily Calorie Targets

Lift Days: 3,200-3,350 calories

Rest Days: 3,000-3,150 calories

Macronutrient Distribution

Protein: 230-260g daily (all days)

Carbs: 350-425g lift | 275-325g rest

Fats: 90-110g lift | 100-120g rest

Nutrient Timing

Pre-Lift (60-90 min before): 40-60g carbs + 25-40g protein

Post-Lift (immediately after): 40g whey protein + fruit

Hydration: 158 oz daily (0.6 oz per lb bodyweight) + electrolytes during heavy sweat sessions

Food Sources

Proteins: Eggs, beef, bison, chicken thighs, salmon, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, jerky

Carbohydrates: Rice, potatoes, oats, fruit, honey, tortillas, rice cakes, pasta

Fats: Olive oil, avocado, nuts, butter, cheese

Thorne Supplement Protocol

Essential Foundation: Creatine 10g morning + 10g evening | D-5000 1 cap daily | Super EPA 2-3 caps daily

Performance Support: Amino Complex around training | Beta Alanine-SR 2 tabs twice daily | Catalyte 1-2 scoops daily

Recovery & Health: Curcumin 4 caps daily | Magnesium Bisglycinate at bedtime

KEY PRINCIPLES

RPE Guide: RPE 7 = 3 reps in reserve | RPE 9 = 1 rep in reserve

Rest Periods: Heavy sets 3-4 min | Supersets 30-45 sec

Auto-Regulation: Sleep less than 8.5 hours = reduce load 15-20% and skip jumps

Pain Management: Remove trigger exercise, substitute similar pattern, or hold 30-45s mid-range isometric
