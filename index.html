COACHING CUES FOR YOUR KEY MOVES

  • Bench: Control down, explode up. Pinch shoulder blades. Don't let hips pop off the pad.
  • Rows: Rigid body, thumbs to ribs, no sagging hips.
  • Box or Back Squat: Sit back to the box, solid brace, explode up.
  • Jumps: Soft landings, tall posture.
  • Power Step + Kick Step: Square hips, quick feet, knee drives.
  • Neck bridge: Stable head, hips high, no overreach.
  • Face Pull: Pull to eyebrow height and rotate out.
  • Pallof: Ribs down, don't rotate. Shoulders square.

SIMPLE GLOSSARY

  • RPE: Effort. 7 = 3 reps left. 9 = 1 rep left.
  • Tempo: 3-1-1 means 3s down, 1s pause, 1s up.
  • Intensity: How heavy the load is vs your max.
  • AMRAP: As many reps as possible with perfect form.
